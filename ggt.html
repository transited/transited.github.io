Golden Gate Transit (AT)

In 2025 I moved back to the Bay Area, landing in Marin. Figuring out the best way to get in and out of the city proved frustrating, toggling back and forth between Golden Gate Transit's very vertical geographical bus map and the associated timetables. So I made a diagram for myself. By the time I was finished it was almost out of date, being overtaken by the MASCOTS plan. But it was a chance to try my hand at an isometric grid, and to get artsy with the Golden Gate Bridge and the Ferry Building. The font is Franklin Gothic (Demi Condensed).
