Ted Rosenbaum

Welcome to my digital anchorage, a counter to my meandering meatspace existence. Currently I'm an expat in Melbourne, where the Moon is upside down the Cubs play in the middle of the night. Before that, I called 5 of the United States' 20 largest regions1 home for varying lengths of time: Chicago, Phildadelphia, DC, San Diego, and the Bay Area.

I'm fascinated by cities and I'm fortunate to be a player with trains and get paid for it. I firmly believe that inertia is the cause of—and solution to all of life's problems2. And that although we should run trains as fast as possible but no faster, they should definitely be more frequent.
