Publications

In addition to the original research below, I've also presented a status update on North American Light Rail & Streetcars at the 13th and 14th National Light Rail Conferences. (2015 in Minneapolis and 2019 in Jersey City, respectively) Towards a Passenger-Focused On-Time Performance Metric for Commuter Rail Paper and Presentation, APTA Rail Conference 2018 This is a paper in 2 parts: (1) tracing the history of the nearly ubiquitous definition of on-time trains being "within 5 minutes of the schedule at the terminal" back to its origins in the steam railroad era of the early 1900s, and (2) a case study using Caltrain comparing the traditional metric with the actual passenger experience during 2016-17. Slides available upon request.
